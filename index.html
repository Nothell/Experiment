Share and Connect

  • In these difficult times, where there has been a surge in Covid cases and deaths around us, research says that there has been a 43% increase in cases of anxiety & depression in India. In an effort to address this we believe that it is very crucial to share and connect. We have come up with an initiative to “Connect & Share”, where you can talk to us about your problems if it makes you feel better. It is a free, small “support group” where you can share personally or in the group about your problems.

#Lets Share, Lets Connect

  • Your stories will help break the stigma around mental health, and inspire others to cope with mental health challenges.

  • We are not trained professionals neither we are offering any medical advice, we are here to listen and simply care for your well-being in ways like giving hope and sharing instances from our life that has worked. We simply want to build a community that is able to support each other.

  • To gather more perspectives on mental health — stories can be from a friend, partner, parent, teacher or mental health professional.
